Mass & gravity relationship


There is a very strong, direct correlation between
the mass of a planet and it's gravitational influence
(r = 0.894)

Density & gravity relationship


There is a moderate, indirect correlation between
the density of a planet and it's gravitational influence
(r = -0.479)

Distance from the sun & orbital velocity relationship


There is a strong, indirect correlation between
the distance a planet is from the sun and it's orbital velocity
(r = -0.768)

Distance from the sun & orbital period relationship


There is a near perfect, direct correlation between
the distance from the sun and a planet's orbital period
(r = 0.988)

Orbital velocity & orbital period relationship


There is a moderate to strong, indirect correlation between
a planet's orbital velocity and orbital period
(r = -0.683)

Gravity & escape velocity relationship


There is a very strong, indirect correlation between
a planet's gravitational influence and required escape velocity
(r = -0.902)
